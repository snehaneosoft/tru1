Bringing Rent Payers & Landlords closer

Ijareed started as a product for renting houses by Rent Payers and earning rewards in return. Over the time it has grown to benefitting both the Rent Payers and the Landlords with the addition of financing like adding Bank Account and applying for Ijareed Credit Card. The aim was to digitalise the rent payment as people today are still paying their rent through cheques without earning anything. The research and planning provides a user centric product with a sustainable value proposition while impacting the industry positively.

The discovered information set the basis for connecting the rent payers and landlords through the digital medium via an app where they can verify their property and identity. Pay and receive rent through the app to have a hassle free experience.

Ijareed Maenaa and Identity

The pillars of the brand that Builds trust

It was vitally crucial for us to create a brand identity that represents our core values and intentions while keeping the market standards intact.

A word that resonates renting in Arabic countries capture the essence of our application. The word Ijareed is a modern adaptation of the word Ijarah that means paying rent in Arabic, reed in the name represents the rewards the user’s will earn after paying or receiving their rent. This revolutionary name signifies the branding value and sheds light on the next generation of paying rent.

Ijareed’s identity is based on the company’s core vision and mission; Secured gateway, pay rent, earn rewards and cashbacks. The “I” in the logo interprets the initials of the company and the circle around it means “Unity in all user’s”. A singular source with a sense of purpose to bring a change in the renting industry.
